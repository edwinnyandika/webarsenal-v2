WebArsenal wasn't built to just scrape data. It was built to empower researchers to understand the full structural complexity of the modern web through automated, high-precision security orchestration.

The Genesis

Born from a need to consolidate fragmented tools, WebArsenal evolved from a simple site-grabber into a 413-module powerhouse. Every script is a result of years of research into web architecture.

The Philosophy

We believe security is composable. By breaking down complex audits into atomic tasks, we allow researchers to build bespoke exfiltration pipelines with surgical precision.
