WebArsenal wasn't built to just scrape data. It was built to empower researchers to understand the full structural complexity of the modern web through automated, high-precision security orchestration.

The Genesis

Born from a need to consolidate fragmented tools, WebArsenal evolved from a simple site-grabber into a 320-module powerhouse. Every script is a result of years of research into web architecture and vulnerability patterns.

The Philosophy

We believe security is com-posable. By breaking down complex audits into atomic tasks—analyzing headers, scraping endpoints, fuzzing parameters—we allow researchers to build bespoke exfiltration pipelines with surgical precision.
